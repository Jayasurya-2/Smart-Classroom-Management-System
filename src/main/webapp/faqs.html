Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about JAAS Instituite of Technology

JAAS Instituite of Technology is an online educational platform dedicated to empowering students with cutting-edge technology skills. We offer courses in programming, web development, and more, along with live classrooms, doubt-clearing sessions, and student support services.

Our courses are open to students, professionals, and anyone interested in learning technology skills. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, we have programs tailored to various skill levels. You need to sign up as a student to access the full range of courses.

Once enrolled and logged in, you can access live classroom sessions through the "Live Classroom" link in the sidebar on the homepage. Schedules are shared in advance, and recordings are available for registered students if you miss a session.

We offer a "Connect for Doubts" feature accessible from the sidebar, linking to a chatbot and faculty support system. Students can submit questions anytime, and our faculty or AI assistant will respond promptly.

After logging in, you can view your progress via the "Student Report" section in the sidebar. It provides detailed insights into your course completion, quiz scores, and overall performance.

Some courses are free, while others require a one-time or subscription-based fee. You can explore the "Courses" section on the homepage for detailed pricing and enrollment options.

You can reach our support team via the "About" page, which includes contact details, or use the "Connect for Doubts" feature for immediate assistance. We’re here to help 24/7!
